Privacy Policy

App: ParkWise
Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Overview

ParkWise ("the App") helps users determine whether parking is allowed at a given location by analyzing photos of parking signs. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Camera & Photos

The App requests access to your device's camera so you can photograph parking signs. Photos you take are sent to our server, which forwards them to the Google Gemini API for analysis. Images are not stored on our server, are transmitted securely over HTTPS, and are used solely to produce a parking analysis result.

No Personal Information

The App does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. No account or registration is required.

Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain device information, such as a device advertising identifier (for example, Apple's IDFA), general (non-precise) location, and app interaction data. On iOS, personalized advertising is used only if you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt; otherwise you will see non-personalized ads. You can learn more and manage choices in Google's Advertising and Privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

Sign images are analyzed using the Google Gemini API, provided by Google LLC. Images sent to Gemini are subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Gemini API Terms of Service. Google may use submitted data in accordance with those policies.

The App also uses Google AdMob (Google LLC) to display advertising. AdMob's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any photos or analysis results. All processing is done in real time and discarded after the result is returned to your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
